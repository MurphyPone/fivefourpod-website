ABOUT

5-4 is a podcast about how much the Supreme Court sucks. Hosted by Peter Shamshiri, Michael Liroff, and Rhiannon Hamam, produced by Rachel Ward, and presented by Slow Burn co-creator Leon Neyfakh. 5-4 is a production of Prologue Projects.

For booking inquiries, please reach out to fivefourpod@gmail.com
